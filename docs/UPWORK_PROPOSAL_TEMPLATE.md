## Pricing Strategies

**For Appointment Booking Projects:**
- Fixed: $2,500-$5,000 (setup + 1 month support)
- Hourly: $75-$150/hour (15-30 hours)

**For Outbound SDR Campaigns:**
- Fixed: $5,000-$10,000 (includes campaign setup, list management, analytics)
- Revenue share: Base + % of booked meetings

**For Support/Inbound Projects:**
- Fixed: $3,000-$6,000 (setup + knowledge base integration)
- Monthly: $1,500-$3,000/month (managed service)

Would you prefer screen share or live phone demo?"

## Red Flags to Avoid

**Don't bid on these:**
- Projects requiring 100+ concurrent calls (need enterprise Vapi plan)
- Projects with $500 budget expecting full system
- "Build me an AI exactly like [famous company]" with no budget
- Clients asking for "just a quick prototype" then scope creeping

## Success Metrics to Highlight

After delivery, showcase:
- Booking rate (% of calls → booked appointments)
- Average handle time
- Sentiment score
- Cost per booked meeting vs human SDR
- 24/7 availability (no human downtime)

Use these metrics in testimonials and case studies for future proposals.
